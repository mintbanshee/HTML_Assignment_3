About Us

To capture what others overlook — that’s the heart of my lens.

LunarLens Photography was born under quiet skies in 2019, founded by a small collective of visual storytellers who believe that the most powerful moments happen in stillness, in shadows, and in the soft light of twilight.

What began as a solo project by a nature-loving night photographer quickly grew into a boutique studio of eight creatives — each bringing their own lens, their own story, and their own way of capturing what others overlook.

From cinematic portraits to surreal nightscapes, LunarLens specializes in images that feel — those that stir emotion, reveal truth, and make the invisible visible.

Based in Ontario and inspired by the rhythm of the natural world, LunarLens serves clients looking for more than a posed photo: we offer a quiet moment, a glimpse of soul, a story told in light.
